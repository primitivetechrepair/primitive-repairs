Game Console Repair FAQs

Common questions before submitting a request.

Do you repair PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch consoles?

Yes. Primitive Tech Repairs can review repair requests for supported PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, and other eligible gaming devices depending on the issue and part availability.

Can HDMI port issues be reviewed?

Yes. Include whether the console has no display, a loose HDMI port, damaged port, bent pins, flickering video, or video output problems.

What overheating issues should I include?

Include whether the console shuts down, shows heat warnings, has loud fan noise, gets hot quickly, or overheats during gameplay.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

Repair pricing depends on the console model, issue type, part availability, and final technician review. Submit a request with your model and issue to begin the process.
