Do you repair cracked back glass?

Yes. Primitive Tech Repairs can review back glass repair requests for supported phones, iPhones, Samsung Galaxy devices, and other eligible devices depending on model and part availability.

Can camera glass damage be reviewed too?

Yes. Include camera glass damage, blurry camera symptoms, rear lens cover damage, or camera area damage when submitting your request.

What should I include in the back glass request?

Include your device model, whether the back glass is cracked or shattered, whether the camera glass is damaged, and whether the device was dropped or exposed to liquid.

Repair pricing depends on the device model, back glass condition, housing damage, part availability, and final technician review. Submit a request with your model and issue to begin the process.
