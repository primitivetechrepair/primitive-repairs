Cookie Policy

This policy explains how Primitive Tech Repairs uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies on our website.

Effective July 23, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files or identifiers stored by a website or service provider on your browser or device.

Similar technologies may include browser local storage, scripts, tags, pixels, and device identifiers.

2. Technologies We Use

Category Purpose Your Choice
Necessary and Security Website operation, preference storage, form protection, fraud prevention, spam prevention, and Google reCAPTCHA. Always active
Analytics Google Analytics may measure visits, traffic sources, pages viewed, and website interactions. Disabled unless you grant analytics permission

3. Necessary and Security Technologies

Necessary technologies support website operation and save your privacy preference.

Google reCAPTCHA is loaded when a protected form requires verification. It may process Internet Protocol address, browser, device, interaction, cookie, and security signals.

Necessary security technologies cannot be disabled through our cookie preference panel because protected forms may not operate safely without them.

4. Google Analytics

Google Analytics may be used to understand how visitors use the website and to improve website performance.

Analytics is not loaded by our consent controller unless you grant analytics permission.

Analytics will remain inactive until a valid Google Analytics measurement ID is configured.

5. Your Cookie Choices

On your first visit, you may accept analytics, reject analytics, or customize your choice.

Your choice is stored in browser local storage under primitiveCookieConsent.

6. Browser Controls

Your browser may allow you to view, block, or delete cookies and website data. Blocking necessary technologies may affect forms, saved preferences, security checks, or other website functionality.

7. Third-Party Technologies

Third-party providers such as Google control their own technology and data-processing practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when technologies, providers, configurations, or legal obligations change.

Questions about cookies or privacy may be submitted through our Contact Us page.
