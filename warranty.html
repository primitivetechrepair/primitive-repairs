Warranty Overview

Warranty coverage is designed to protect eligible repairs.

Primitive Tech Repairs provides warranty coverage on eligible parts and labor. Warranty coverage applies only to the original repair performed and does not cover new physical damage, liquid damage, misuse, software-related issues, or repairs performed by another party after service.

Coverage Details

Warranty coverage guidelines.

Covered Situations

  • Issue is related to the exact repair service performed.
  • Replacement part failure within the warranty period.
  • Labor issue connected to the original repair.
  • No new physical, liquid, or third-party damage found.

Not Covered By Warranty

  • New cracks, drops, pressure damage, bends, or dents.
  • New LCD/OLED damage, black spots, colored lines, bleeding, pressure marks, or internal display damage caused after repair.
  • Liquid exposure after the repair.
  • Software, account, settings, charger, or accessory issues.
  • Repair attempts by another person or business.
Warranty FAQs

Common questions about repair warranty coverage.

How long is the warranty period?

Most eligible repairs include a 1-Year Warranty covering the original repair performed.

Does the warranty cover accidental damage?

No. New cracks, drops, bends, pressure damage, liquid damage, and other accidental damage are not covered.

Does opening the device elsewhere affect warranty coverage?

Yes. Warranty coverage may be void if another repair provider attempts repair after the original service.

Do I need proof of repair?

Repair records help verify warranty eligibility and speed up claim processing.
