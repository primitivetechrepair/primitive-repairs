Common questions before submitting a request.

Do you repair phone cameras?

Yes. Primitive Tech Repairs can review camera repair requests for supported phones, iPhones, Samsung Galaxy devices, tablets, and other eligible devices.

Can cracked camera glass be reviewed?

Yes. Include cracked camera glass, damaged lens cover glass, blurry images, scratches, camera ring damage, or rear camera area damage in your request.

What camera issues should I include?

Include whether the camera is blurry, blacked out, shaking, not focusing, showing an error, failing in the camera app, or affected after a drop.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

Repair pricing depends on the device model, camera condition, glass damage, part availability, and final technician review. Submit a request with your model and issue to begin the process.
