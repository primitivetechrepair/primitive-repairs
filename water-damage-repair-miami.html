Water Damage Repair FAQs

Common questions before submitting a request.

Do you review water damaged phones?

Yes. Primitive Tech Repairs can review water damage repair requests for supported phones, iPhones, Samsung Galaxy devices, tablets, computers, consoles, and other eligible devices.

What should I include in the request?

Include what liquid the device was exposed to, when it happened, whether the device powers on, whether it charges, and any screen, camera, speaker, or performance symptoms.

Should I keep using the device after water damage?

Avoid heavy use or charging if the device may still have moisture inside. Continued use can make liquid damage worse. Submit a request so the issue can be reviewed.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

Repair pricing depends on the device model, liquid exposure, corrosion level, part availability, and final technician review. Submit a request with your model and issue to begin the process.
