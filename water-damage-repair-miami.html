Water Damage Repair FAQs

Answers to common questions about water damage repair services.

Do you repair water damaged devices?

Yes. We support water damage repair services for phones, iPhones, Samsung Galaxy devices, tablets, computers, game consoles, and other supported electronics.

What should I include in the request?

Include what liquid the device was exposed to, when it happened, whether the device powers on, whether it charges, and any screen, camera, speaker, or performance symptoms.

Should I keep using the device after water damage?

Avoid heavy use or charging if the device may still have moisture inside. Continued use can make liquid damage worse. Have the device inspected as soon as possible to help prevent additional corrosion or component damage.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

Repair pricing depends on the device model, liquid exposure, corrosion level, parts availability, and repairs required. Once we review your device details, we'll provide available repair options and pricing information.

Can water damage cause a device to stop charging?

Yes. Liquid exposure can damage charging ports, batteries, charging circuits, and motherboard components. Charging problems are one of the most common symptoms after water damage.

Do you offer a warranty on water damage repairs?

Yes. Eligible water damage repairs include warranty coverage. Warranty terms may vary depending on the severity of the damage and repairs performed.
