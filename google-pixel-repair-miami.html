Google Pixel Repair FAQs

Answers to common questions about Google Pixel repair services.

Do you repair Google Pixel screens?

Yes. We help with cracked screens, display issues, touch problems, display lines, black spots, and OLED-related issues on supported Google Pixel models.

Do you replace Google Pixel batteries?

Yes. We help with battery drain, unexpected shutdowns, charging issues, battery swelling, overheating, and battery replacement services for supported Pixel devices.

Do you repair Google Pixel USB-C charging ports?

Yes. We help with charging port issues, loose connections, charging interruptions, damaged USB-C ports, and devices that no longer charge properly.

Do you repair water-damaged Google Pixel phones?

Yes. We review water-damaged Pixel devices for liquid exposure, corrosion, charging issues, display problems, and no-power symptoms to determine available repair options.

What Pixel model details should I include?

Include the exact Pixel model, issue symptoms, when the issue started, whether the phone powers on or charges, and any photos or videos that help explain the problem.

Is pricing confirmed before repair work begins?

Yes. Submitting a request does not automatically approve repair work. Once we review your device details, we'll provide available repair options, pricing information, and next steps before service begins.

Do you offer a warranty on Google Pixel repairs?

Yes. Eligible repairs include warranty coverage. Warranty terms may vary depending on the repair performed and replacement parts used.
