USB-C Port Repair FAQs

Answers to common questions about USB-C charging port repair.

Do you repair USB-C charging ports?

Yes. We support USB-C charging port repair services for phones, tablets, Nintendo Switch consoles, laptops, and other supported devices.

What are common signs of USB-C port damage?

Common signs include no charging, slow charging, loose cable fit, charging only at an angle, intermittent connection, bent pins, debris inside the port, or visible connector damage.

Is USB-C port repair considered microsoldering?

Some USB-C repairs are simple part or port replacements, but many USB-C ports are soldered to the board and may require microsoldering, especially when pads, traces, or board connections are damaged.

Can a Nintendo Switch USB-C port be repaired?

We support Nintendo Switch USB-C repair services for no-charge issues, dock connection problems, loose ports, and charging or video output failures.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

USB-C repair pricing depends on the device model, charging port condition, board condition, parts availability, and repair complexity.

Do you offer a warranty on USB-C port repairs?

Yes. Eligible USB-C charging port repairs include warranty coverage. Warranty terms may vary depending on the repair performed and replacement parts used.

Can damaged USB-C pins be repaired?

Yes. Bent pins, damaged connectors, broken port housings, and charging failures caused by physical USB-C port damage can often be repaired or replaced depending on the device.
