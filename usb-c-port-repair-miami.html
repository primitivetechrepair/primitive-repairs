USB-C Port Repair FAQs

Common questions before submitting a request.

Do you repair USB-C charging ports?

Yes. Primitive Tech Repairs can review USB-C port repair requests for supported phones, tablets, Nintendo Switch consoles, laptops, and other eligible devices.

What are common signs of USB-C port damage?

Common signs include no charging, slow charging, loose cable fit, charging only at an angle, intermittent connection, bent pins, debris inside the port, or visible connector damage.

Is USB-C port repair considered microsoldering?

Some USB-C repairs are simple part or port replacements, but many USB-C ports are soldered to the board and may require microsoldering, especially when pads, traces, or board connections are damaged.

Can a Nintendo Switch USB-C port be repaired?

Primitive Tech Repairs can review Nintendo Switch USB-C repair requests for no-charge issues, dock connection problems, loose ports, and related charging or video output symptoms.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

USB-C repair pricing depends on the device model, port condition, board condition, part availability, and technician review. Submit a request with your device model and charging issue to begin.
