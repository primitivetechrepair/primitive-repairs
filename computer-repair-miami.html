Computer Repair FAQs

Common questions before submitting a request.

Do you repair laptops and desktops?

Yes. Primitive Tech Repairs can review computer repair requests for supported laptops, desktops, PCs, Macs, and other eligible computer devices.

What computer issues should I include?

Include whether the computer has screen damage, charging problems, overheating, startup failure, keyboard issues, slow performance, liquid damage, or unknown errors.

Can overheating or power issues be reviewed?

Yes. Include symptoms like loud fan noise, heat warnings, random shutdowns, failure to power on, boot loops, or charging-related problems.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

Repair pricing depends on the computer model, issue type, part availability, condition of the device, and final technician review. Submit a request with your model and issue to begin the process.
