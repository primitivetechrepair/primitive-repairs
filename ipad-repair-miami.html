iPad Repair FAQs

Common questions before submitting a request.

Do you repair iPad screens?

Yes. Primitive Tech Repairs can review iPad screen repair requests for cracked glass, touch problems, display lines, black spots, LCD issues, and other screen-related symptoms.

Can I submit an iPad battery replacement request?

Yes. You can submit a request for iPad battery drain, charging issues, shutdowns, swollen battery concerns, or general power-related review.

Do you review iPad charging port problems?

Yes. Include whether the iPad will not charge, charges slowly, only charges at an angle, has USB-C or Lightning port problems, or has visible charging port damage.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

Repair pricing depends on the iPad model, selected repair, part availability, condition of the device, and final technician review. Submit a request with your model and issue to begin the process.
