iPad Repair FAQs

Answers to common questions about iPad repair services.

Do you repair iPad screens?

Yes. We help with cracked screens, touch issues, display lines, black spots, LCD problems, and other screen-related issues on supported iPad models.

Do you replace iPad batteries?

Yes. We help with battery drain, unexpected shutdowns, charging issues, battery swelling, overheating, and battery replacement services for supported iPad models.

Do you repair iPad charging ports?

Yes. We help with Lightning and USB-C charging port issues, loose connections, charging interruptions, and devices that no longer charge properly.

Do you repair water-damaged iPads?

Yes. We review water-damaged iPads for liquid exposure, charging issues, display problems, no-power symptoms, and corrosion-related concerns to determine available repair options.

Pricing depends on the iPad model, repair type, device condition, and parts required. Once we review your repair details, we'll provide available repair options, pricing information, and next steps before service begins.

Do you offer a warranty on iPad repairs?

Yes. Eligible repairs include warranty coverage. Warranty terms may vary depending on the repair performed and replacement parts used.
