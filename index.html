Primitive Tech Repair — Repair Request Policy

By submitting a repair request, you agree to the following:

  1. Diagnosis & Inspection: All repair requests are subject to inspection. The issue described may not be the only issue found.
  2. Repair Approval: No paid repair work will begin until the customer approves the repair quote, estimated cost, and service details.
  3. Data Responsibility: Customers are responsible for backing up their device before service. Primitive Tech Repair is not responsible for lost data, software issues, passwords, photos, apps, files, or account locks.
  4. Device Condition: Primitive Tech Repair is not responsible for pre-existing damage, hidden internal damage, liquid damage, board-level damage, prior repair damage, or issues discovered during inspection.
  5. Parts & Warranty: Warranty coverage depends on the repair type and part used. Warranty does not cover physical damage, liquid damage, misuse, tampering, new unrelated issues, or damage caused after repair.
  6. Turnaround Time: Estimated repair times are not guaranteed. Delays may occur due to parts, diagnostics, shipping, customer response time, or repair complexity.
  7. Service Options: Customers must provide accurate contact and service information. Meet-up locations should be public or business locations. Mail-in instructions will be provided after review.
  8. Photos & Attachments: Uploaded photos/videos are used only to help review the repair request and understand the device condition.
  9. Customer Consent: By submitting this request, you confirm the information provided is accurate and authorize Primitive Tech Repair to contact you regarding your repair request.
