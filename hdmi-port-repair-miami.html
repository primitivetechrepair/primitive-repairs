HDMI Port Repair FAQs

Common questions before submitting a request.

Do you repair HDMI ports on game consoles?

Yes. Primitive Tech Repairs can review HDMI port repair requests for supported consoles, including PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch-related video output issues, and other eligible devices.

What are common signs of HDMI port damage?

Common signs include no signal, black screen, loose HDMI connection, bent pins, intermittent display, visible port damage, or a console that powers on but does not show video.

Is HDMI repair considered microsoldering?

Many HDMI port repairs require board-level work because the port is soldered to the motherboard. If pads or traces are damaged, additional microsoldering diagnostics may be needed.

Can every HDMI port be repaired?

Not always. Repair options depend on the console model, board condition, missing pads, previous repair attempts, corrosion, and whether the board damage is practical to repair.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

HDMI repair pricing depends on the device model, port condition, board condition, part availability, and technician review. Submit a request with your model and issue to begin.
