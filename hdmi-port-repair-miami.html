HDMI Port Repair FAQs

Answers to common questions about HDMI port repair services.

Do you repair HDMI ports on game consoles?

Yes. We support HDMI port repair requests for PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, and other supported devices experiencing video output issues.

What are common signs of HDMI port damage?

Common symptoms include no signal messages, black screens, loose HDMI connections, bent pins, intermittent display output, visible port damage, or a console that powers on without displaying video.

Is HDMI repair considered microsoldering?

Many HDMI repairs involve microsoldering because the HDMI port is soldered directly to the motherboard. Additional board-level repair may be required if pads, traces, or solder joints are damaged.

Can every HDMI port be repaired?

Not always. Repair options depend on the console model, board condition, previous repair attempts, corrosion, missing pads, and the extent of motherboard damage.

Do you repair PS5 HDMI ports?

Yes. We support HDMI repair requests for PlayStation 5 consoles experiencing no signal, black screen issues, damaged ports, and video output failures.

Do you repair Xbox HDMI ports?

Yes. We support HDMI repair requests for Xbox consoles experiencing damaged ports, display failures, and no signal symptoms.

Is pricing confirmed before repair work begins?

Yes. Submitting a request does not automatically approve repair work. Once we review your device details, we'll provide available repair options, pricing information, and next steps before service begins.

Do you offer a warranty on HDMI repairs?

Yes. Eligible repairs include warranty coverage. Warranty terms may vary depending on the repair performed and the condition of the device.
