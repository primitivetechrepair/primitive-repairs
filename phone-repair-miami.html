Phone Repair FAQs

Answers to common questions about phone repair services.

Do you repair iPhone and Samsung phones?

Yes. We support repair requests for iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, Motorola, and many other smartphone models. Select your exact device in the repair wizard so we can review available repair options.

Can I request phone repair near me in Miami?

Yes. Customers throughout the Miami service area can submit a repair request online. Available service options depend on your device, repair type, location, scheduling, and parts availability.

What phone issues can I submit?

We help with cracked screens, battery problems, charging port issues, back glass damage, camera failures, water damage, no-power symptoms, software concerns, and device diagnostics.

Do you repair USB-C and Lightning charging ports?

Yes. We support repair requests for USB-C and Lightning charging port issues, including damaged ports, loose connections, intermittent charging, and devices that no longer charge properly.

Is pricing confirmed before repair work begins?

Yes. Submitting a request does not automatically approve repair work. Once we review your device details, we'll provide available repair options, pricing information, and next steps before service begins.

Do you offer a warranty on phone repairs?

Yes. Eligible repairs include warranty coverage. Warranty terms may vary depending on the repair performed and replacement parts used.
