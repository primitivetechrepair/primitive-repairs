Charging Port Repair FAQs

Common questions before submitting a request.

Do you repair phone charging ports?

Yes. Primitive Tech Repairs can review charging port repair requests for supported phones, iPhones, Samsung Galaxy devices, tablets, and other eligible devices.

What charging issues should I include in the request?

Include whether the device will not charge, charges slowly, disconnects easily, only charges at an angle, has visible port damage, or has been exposed to liquid.

Can debris cause charging problems?

Yes. Dust, lint, debris, cable damage, and port wear can all affect charging. A technician review helps determine whether cleaning, repair, or replacement is needed.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

Repair pricing depends on the device model, port condition, part availability, and final technician review. Submit a request with your model and issue to begin the review process.
