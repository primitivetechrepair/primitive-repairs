Battery Replacement FAQs

Answers to common questions about battery replacement services.

Do you replace phone batteries?

Yes. We support battery replacement services for phones, iPhones, Samsung Galaxy devices, Google Pixel devices, tablets, and other supported electronics.

What battery issues should I include in the request?

Include whether the battery drains quickly, overheats, swells, shuts down randomly, charges slowly, or no longer holds a charge.

Is a swollen battery serious?

Yes. A swollen battery can create pressure inside the device and may become a safety concern. Avoid continued use and have the device inspected as soon as possible.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

Pricing depends on the device model, battery availability, device condition, and parts required. Once we review your repair details, we'll provide available service options and pricing information.

Do you offer a warranty on battery replacements?

Yes. Eligible battery replacement services include warranty coverage. Warranty terms may vary depending on the device and replacement battery used.
