Battery Replacement FAQs

Common questions before submitting a request.

Do you replace phone batteries?

Yes. Primitive Tech Repairs can review battery replacement requests for supported phones, iPhones, Samsung Galaxy devices, tablets, and other eligible devices.

What battery issues should I include in the request?

Include whether the battery drains quickly, overheats, swells, shuts down randomly, charges slowly, or no longer holds a charge.

Is a swollen battery serious?

Yes. A swollen battery can create pressure inside the device and should be reviewed carefully. Avoid pressing on the device or continuing heavy use until it is inspected.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

Repair pricing depends on the device model, battery availability, device condition, and final technician review. Submit a request with your model and issue to begin the review process.
