Screen Repair FAQs

Answers to common questions about screen repair services.

Do you repair cracked phone screens?

Yes. We help with cracked glass, touch problems, display lines, black spots, screen flickering, ghost touch issues, and OLED or LCD-related problems.

Do you repair iPhone and Samsung screens?

Yes. We support screen repair requests for iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, Motorola, tablets, and other supported devices.

What screen issues should I include in my request?

Include whether the screen is cracked, blacked out, flickering, showing lines, bleeding, displaying black spots, experiencing ghost touch, or not responding to touch.

Do you repair OLED and LCD screens?

Yes. We support repair requests involving OLED and LCD displays depending on the device model and parts availability.

Is pricing confirmed before repair work begins?

Yes. Submitting a request does not automatically approve repair work. Once we review your device details, we'll provide available repair options, pricing information, and next steps before service begins.

Do you offer a warranty on screen repairs?

Yes. Eligible repairs include warranty coverage. Warranty terms may vary depending on the repair performed and replacement parts used.
