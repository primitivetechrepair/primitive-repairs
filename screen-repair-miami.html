Screen Repair FAQs

Common questions before submitting a request.

Do you repair cracked phone screens?

Yes. Primitive Tech Repairs can review screen repair requests for cracked glass, touch problems, display lines, black spots, flickering, and OLED/LCD-related symptoms.

Do you repair iPhone and Samsung screens?

Yes. You can submit screen repair requests for supported iPhone models, Samsung Galaxy devices, tablets, and other eligible devices depending on part availability and inspection.

What screen issues should I include in the request?

Include whether the screen is cracked, blacked out, showing lines, bleeding, flickering, not responding to touch, showing black spots, or has pressure damage.

Is final pricing shown before inspection?

Repair pricing depends on the device model, selected screen repair, part availability, and final technician review. Submit a request with your model and issue to begin the review process.
